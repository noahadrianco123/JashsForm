STRENGTHENED SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

There are 5 Core Subjects required for any SHS learner (Acad Track or TechPro Track).

ACADEMIC CLUSTERS

ARTS, SOCIAL SCIENCES & HUMANITIES (ASSH)

  • Creative Industries (Visual, Literary, Media, Applied, and Traditional Art)
  • Creative Writing
  • Ethics: The Filipino Identity through the Arts
  • Introduction to the Philosophy of the Human Person
  • Malikhaing Pagsulat
  • Philippine Politics and Governance
  • Wika at Komunikasyon sa Akademikong Filipino

SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING AND MATHEMATICS (STEM)

  • Advanced Mathematies 1-2
  • Biology 1 - 2,3-4
  • Chemistry 1 - 2,3 - 4
  • Empowerment Technologies
    • Fundamentals of Data Analytics and Management
  • Pre-Calculus 1 -2
  • Physics 1 - 2, 3-4
  • Trigonometry 1-2

BUSINESS AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP (BE)

  • Basic Accounting
  • Business Finance and Income Taxation
  • Contemporary Marketing and Business Economics
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Introduction to Organization and Management
  • Pre-Calculus 1 -2
  • Physics 1 - 2, 3-4
  • Trigonometry 1-2

FIELD EXPERIENCE

  • Arts Apprenticeship - Literary Arts
  • Arts Apprenticeship - Visual, Media, Applied, and Traditional Art
  • Research Methods
  • Work Immersion
